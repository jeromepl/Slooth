About Slooth

To use Slooth, simply click "Record New Macro" and Slooth will start recording all your actions after the first redirect that occurs.
Once you click "Stop Recording", you will be prompted to enter a name for the new macro.

Note that Slooth currently does not work very well with websites that load asynchronously as it attempts to run the recorded actions as fast as possible.

Slooth was initially built by a team of 4 at the 2016 McHacks hackathon where it placed first!

Contribute to Slooth on the project's github page.
